RE: How can attorneys use AI to benefit their practice?

## QUESTION PRESENTED: 
How can a non-technical attorney use new AI tools to increase their productivity and become more advanced advocates?

## SHORT ANSWER: 
AI can help attorneys with a wide range of tasks from legal research to document review and even investigation. Understanding your options and the limitations of the current technology can immediately improve your practice and save you time.
## DISCUSSION

Outline:
- AI can and will help you complete repetitive tasks, gather information quickly, and analyze large batches of documents quickly. AI will add value if you know how to use it
- AI Agents are comparable to a colleague. It is an enhancement
